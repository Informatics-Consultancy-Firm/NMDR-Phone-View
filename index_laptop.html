National Malaria Data Repository

A centralized, integrated platform for malaria surveillance, programme monitoring, and data-driven decision making across Sierra Leone.

About the Repository
What
What is the NMDR?
  • A national digital infrastructure for malaria programme data
  • Hosted on DHIS2 integrated with HMIS, LMIS
  • Developed: Informatics Consultancy Firm — Sierra Leone (ICF-SL)
  • Technical Support: WHO, CHAI, AHADI, AMMnet-SL
  • Funded: The Global Fund
Why
Why the NMDR Exists
  • Consolidate fragmented malaria data into one repository
  • Eliminate data silos between systems
  • Strengthen national data governance
  • Support data analytics at national and sub-national levels
  • Improve data quality for decision-making
Focus
Focus Areas
  • Malaria Surveillance
  • Supply Chain Management
  • Interventions
  • Resources and Documentation
  • Automated Pipelines
  • Analytics
Benefits
Expected Benefits
  • Faster evidence-based programme planning and reporting
  • Improved data quality through validation
  • Enhanced transparency and accountability
  • Early warning signs for stockouts and outbreaks
