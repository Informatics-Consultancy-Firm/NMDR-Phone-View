National Malaria Data Repository

A centralized, integrated platform for malaria surveillance, programme monitoring, and data-driven decision making across Sierra Leone.

About the Repository
What
What is the NMDR?
  • A national digital infrastructure for malaria programme data
  • Hosted on DHIS2 integrated with HMIS, LMIS
  • Developed by NMCP, ICF-SL, WHO, CHAI
Why
Why the NMDR Exists
  • Consolidate fragmented malaria data into one repository
  • Eliminate data silos between systems
  • Strengthen national data governance
Focus
Focus Areas
  • Case management — testing, treatment, reporting
  • Vector control — IRS coverage, ITN distribution
  • Malaria in pregnancy — IPTp, ANC attendance
Benefits
Expected Benefits
  • Faster evidence-based programme planning
  • Improved data quality through validation
  • Enhanced transparency and accountability
