How It Works
1
Fetch Facility Registry
Loads all Level-5 org units from DHIS2 with district and chiefdom hierarchy. Each facility is classified by name suffix into one of 5 assessed types. All other types are excluded.
TypeLevel
HospitalLevel 5
ClinicLevel 5
CHCLevel 5
CHPLevel 5
MCHPLevel 5
2
Data Elements and Datasets
Six data elements are queried: confirmed positives (RDT Pos + Microscopy Pos for facility, RDT Pos for community) and treated (ACT under 24h + over 24h for facility, ACT under 24h + over 24h for community HTR). Microscopy is excluded for CHP and MCHP.
3
Analytics Query
All 18 de.coc pairs are sent in a single dx dimension with periods batched at 24 and ou:LEVEL-5 covering all facilities in one request.
4
TPR Computation
TPR = (Confirmed / Tested) × 100 per age group per facility per month. Confirmed = RDT Pos + Microscopy Pos (Microscopy excluded for CHP/MCHP). Tested = RDT Pos + RDT Neg + Microscopy Pos + Microscopy Neg (last two excluded for CHP/MCHP). Community = RDT Pos / (RDT Pos + RDT Neg) × 100.
5
Map No-Data Categories
Geographic maps distinguish three no-data states. "No Tested" means a facility tested but had no positives. "No Confirmed" means treated cases with no confirmed cases. "No Confirmed, No Tested" means no data reported.
