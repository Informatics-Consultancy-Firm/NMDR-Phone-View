Integrated suite of digital tools for malaria campaign management, surveillance, and data quality across Sierra Leone.

📱 SBD App Features

  • Multi-role PWA (ICF Collect, ITN Movement, ID Card Generators)
  • School location cascading selection with CSV backend

  • AI-powered chat agent for school targeting
  • QR code generation for school identification
  • Staff ID card generation with bulk upload
  • Knowledge assessment tools for training validation
  • ITN distribution movement tracking with DMS logistics
  • Duplicate blocking and quality assurance features
