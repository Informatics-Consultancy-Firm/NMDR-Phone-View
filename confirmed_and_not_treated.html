How It Works
1
Fetch Facility Registry
Loads all Level-5 org units from DHIS2 with district and chiefdom hierarchy. Each facility is classified by name suffix into one of 5 assessed types. All other types are excluded.

2
Data Elements and Datasets
Six data elements are queried: confirmed positives (RDT Pos + Microscopy Pos for facility, RDT Pos for community) and treated (ACT under 24h + over 24h for facility, ACT under 24h + over 24h for community HTR). Microscopy is excluded for CHP and MCHP.

3
Analytics Query
All 18 de.coc pairs are sent (6 DEs × 3 age COCs each — confirmed + treated) in a single dx dimension with periods batched at 24 and ou:LEVEL-5 covering all facilities in one request. Level-6 CHW and ETR records are automatically rolled up to their Level-5 MCHP parent.
Parameter Value
Endpoint/api/analytics.json
dimension dx6 de.coc pairs (facility + community)
dimension peSelected months (batched at 24)
dimension ouLEVEL-5
API callsTypically 1 to 2 per run
4
Proportion Computation
Confirmed but Not Treated = Confirmed − min(Confirmed, Treated) per age group per facility per month. This gives the number of confirmed cases that did not receive treatment. If Treated ≥ Confirmed the result is 0.
Indicator Source Formula
% 0 to 59 monthsFacilitymax(0, confAge0 − treatAge0) / total × 100
Communitymax(0, confComAge0 − treatComAge0) / total × 100
% 5 to 14 yearsFacilitymax(0, confAge1 − treatAge1) / total × 100
Communitymax(0, confComAge1 − treatComAge1) / total × 100
% 15 years and aboveFacilitymax(0, confAge2 − treatAge2) / total × 100
Communitymin(confComAge2, treatComAge2) / total × 100
Facility shareCombinedfacTotal / (facTotal + commTotal) × 100
Community shareCombinedcommTotal / (facTotal + commTotal) × 100
5
Outputs
Builds 7 proportion charts, geographic monthly and annual maps across Hospital, Clinic, CHC, CHP and MCHP, a facility tracker, summary table and downloadable Excel. Microscopy confirmed excluded for CHP and MCHP.
